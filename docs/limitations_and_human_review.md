# Limitations and Human Review

## Limitations

EAS does not guarantee:

- design certification
- code compliance
- safety approval
- licensed engineering review
- root-cause certainty
- complete evidence extraction
- complete document interpretation
- valid use of missing or ambiguous inputs
- production readiness

## Human Review Required

Qualified human review is required for:

- life safety
- structural, pressure, electrical, fire, medical, aviation, or public infrastructure decisions
- regulated or code-governed systems
- production changes
- incomplete data
- ambiguous documents
- unsupported deterministic computation
- conflicting diagnostic evidence
- any recommendation before implementation

## What Is Not Included

This document does not include private risk classifiers, legal advice, professional engineering certification, or operational approval.
